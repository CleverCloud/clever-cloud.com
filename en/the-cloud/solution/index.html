A few words about Clever Cloud :

Clever Cloud is a PaaS (Platform as a Service) hosting service. You deploy your app easily, without any configuration.

You no longer have to mange virtual machines, or asking yourself about your performance needs: these settings are automatically handled by Clever Cloud.

Focus on things that matter : your app.

Performances & technologies

Clever Cloud handle your code or project without conditions. Integrate your application within the platform in a dead simple way, enjoying the benefits of a cloud infrastructure.

  • Zen integration
  • Multi-languages support
  • high availability cloud hosting
  • Advanced peering

Controlled consumption

Clever Cloud brings a new vision of the economic model of hosting, thanks to the "Drop".

  • Pay as you go
  • One single unit : the Drop

Simple management of your apps and services

You manage your remaining Drops on our real-time dashboard, Clever Cloud manages everything else for your application.

  • Self-service
  • High-end security
  • Secure and blazing fast deploy
  • 24/7 real-time management
