A few words about Clever Cloud :

Clever Cloud is a PaaS (Platform as a Service) hosting service. With our help, you can deploy your app directly without going through a configuration stage.

You no longer have to manage virtual machines or calculate your performance needs, as these features are handled automatically and cost-efficiently by Clever Cloud.

Thanks to Clever Cloud, you can focus on what actually matters : your app.

Performances and technologies

Clever cloud handles your code or your project with no conditions. The integration process is dead simple and will give you the benefit of a cloud infrastructure :

Controlled Consumption

The whole Clever Cloud system revolves around a revolutionary billing system : the Drop. Each Drop corresponds to a certain amount of computing energy, and it allows you to have a clear view of your actual consumption

Simple management of apps and services
